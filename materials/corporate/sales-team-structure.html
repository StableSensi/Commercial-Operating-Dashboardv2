The team

One team around one pipeline: a sales lead who closes, two junior sellers who drive the deal flow, a marketing producer and an office manager — clear positions, shared number.

Owner: Sales & Marketing · Confidential

How the department is built.

The department runs as one team with clear positions. Everyone works toward the same commercial number; what differs is what each person carries. The sales lead carries the close — the qualified conversations, the negotiations, the merchant agreements. The junior sellers carry the engine that feeds it: they own the lead flow from conference lists, cold outreach and inbound through qualification, call setup and follow-up, and they sit in the warm conversations as the sales lead’s right hand in the daily deal work.

The positions are deliberate. The junior sellers hold real commercial ground — the discipline that keeps every deal moving lives with them, and they grow inside the live deals, not beside them. Marketing production keeps Aryze sharp externally; office & operations keeps the practical machine running. Four positions, one motion.
