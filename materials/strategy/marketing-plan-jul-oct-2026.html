Marketing plan · Jul–Oct 2026

Marketing plan

Lead generation and conversion for the whole period — Pay by Bank in the live UK market, the EU/EEA added gradually from July once technical go-live on the Volt extension is confirmed, plus one institutional Digital Cash track.

Owner: Marketing · Prepared 11 June 2026 · Confidential

The plan in one line

Sized to support the 2026 Objectives — >20 UK casinos live 1 Aug, 50 EU live 1 Sep, >140 live by Dec, >840k EUR monthly revenue by Dec. Marketing owns the leading indicators below; sales and onboarding own live conversion. Scale logic: platform deals — one platform, many casinos.

Two tracks

Pay by Bank — core

High-risk merchants, UK + EU/EEA

Primarily iGaming, casino and betting, 100k+ transactions a month, where card cost, settlement delay, chargeback exposure or payment cost materially affects margin. Buyer: the Payments Operations Lead. UK live and sellable; EU merchants get a live sandbox now and can start building immediately, no waiting for go-live. Anchor message: "Get paid faster, with less friction and more control."

Digital Cash — round-table track

Tier-2 banks

Already-licensed institutions where Aryze provides the infrastructure layer and the bank remains issuer and operator under its own licence. The September round table builds pipeline; it does not close deals this quarter.

Strategic frame

The north star is more than 4.2M monthly casino transactions by 1 December 2026, carried by 40+ UK and 100+ EU live casinos. The plan feeds that roadmap month by month: EU operating roof (July) → first UK live base (August) → EU launch at scale (September) → revenue checkpoint (October). Marketing's contribution is measured in qualified conversations, EU pipeline and platform routes — live conversion is owned by sales and onboarding.

Operating mode. The remaining education assets (AMA, "Learn Pay by Bank" video series) are produced and shipped in July, then retired as an active workstream. From August, the blog series carries the educational load as SEO and credibility support. Around the anchors: a Sushi Night for target accounts at iGB L!VE (1 July), monthly partner workshops, a ticket giveaway feeding the newsletter, two blogs a month, the July video series, and a short paid push from 1 August.
