UK proves transaction density. EU creates the scale curve.

The plan separates two jobs. UK execution is about disciplined conversion of a known pipeline into active transaction volume. EU execution starts with licensing coverage, then turns a much larger pipeline into a live casino base with meaningful volume.

UK motion

Transaction density from a focused pipeline

The September volume target is more than 600k UK transactions per month. 20+ of 30 casinos live.

EU motion

Scale curve from licensing coverage

The EU objective is 50 live casinos by September, then 100+ live by November. 50 live by September.

Revenue motion

Volume translated into recurring revenue

October becomes the first revenue checkpoint, then December doubles the target. 840k EUR monthly target.
