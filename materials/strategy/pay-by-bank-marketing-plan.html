Marketing plan · Pay by Bank · April–May 2026

Build trust first. Convert second.

The Pay by Bank campaign educates high-risk merchants on the true cost of card processing and positions Aryze as a faster, cheaper and more reliable account-to-account payment infrastructure partner.

Lead generation through education and credibility.

High-risk merchants often pay materially more for card processing through fees, failed payments, reserves, chargebacks and FX friction. The campaign makes that cost visible, then presents Pay by Bank as the direct, regulated and operationally simpler alternative.

ICP

iGaming operators, crypto exchanges and online marketplaces. Decision-makers with payment pain, complex settlement needs and high cost sensitivity.

Positioning

"Danish-built payment infrastructure for serious merchants." Regulatory seriousness, network reliability and operational clarity are the trust anchors.

Conversion

The path runs from education to a demo or setup conversation. Every asset carries a next step: demo, webinar, roundtable or free setup sign-up.

Each channel has a specific job.

SEO / Blog

Own the education layer

Publish explainers around Pay by Bank, card-cost pain and high-risk merchant payment flows.

LinkedIn

Build visible authority

Use organic posts, sponsored reach and founder/team content to reach ICP decision-makers.

Brevo

Nurture and convert

Use newsletter and email flows to move readers toward demo calls, event attendance and setup.

Events

Create qualified conversations

Use roundtables, conference anchors and themed moments such as Bitcoin Pizza Day.
