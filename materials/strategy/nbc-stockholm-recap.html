What landed, what to sharpen

What went well

  • Panel credibility. Sharing the stage with BlackRock was a standout credibility moment and our best visibility of the event.
  • Prime booth location. A high-traffic spot kept a steady flow of visitors and conversations.
  • EKR.dk demo at the booth. The live demo drew a lot of questions from visitors.
  • Right crowd. A largely blockchain / crypto-native audience kept conversations relevant — and surfaced real Pay by Bank use cases.
  • Clean lead capture. Every required field captured, so each lead is ready for segmented follow-up.

What we'd sharpen

  • Lead volume. Sign-ups came in modest, below the 50 target, despite strong footfall.
  • Buyer mix. We'd welcome more payments and traditional-finance buyers in the room next edition.
  • On-floor demos. A tighter, repeatable Pay by Bank demo would help convert booth interest faster.

Follow-up so far: a thank-you email went out to all 35 leads via Brevo last week, inviting them to follow Aryze on social.

Outlook for Aryze

Pipeline

All 35 leads captured and sent a thank-you email via Brevo last week.

Credibility

The BlackRock panel plus a relevant lead list is a strong base for Q3 outreach.

Partnership

NBC is a high-fit event for Aryze; worth a conversation about scaling our presence — ideally with more payments-side attendees.
