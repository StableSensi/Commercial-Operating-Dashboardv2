The position

UK and European merchants. One integration, one Payment Initiation Service Provider (PISP) framework, 19 markets via Mastercard Open Banking rails.

What we cover

Pay by Bank is incoming deposits, top-ups, and checkout payments — customer to merchant. The player pushes money directly from their bank to yours. No card, no scheme, no chargeback path.

  • Player selects Pay by Bank in your existing checkout
  • Authenticates inside their bank app or web (SCA at the bank — no 3DS pop-up on your site)
  • Funds land, irrevocable, bank-to-bank

PSD2-licensed PISP rail via Mastercard Open Banking. No card data ever touches your checkout.

What changes for your deposit P&L

Fixed fee per transaction. Cost stops scaling with deposit size. UK account-to-account (A2A) pricing has reached GBP 0.50 per transaction at scale; card rails charge a share of every deposit. Scheme fees have risen materially for large merchants in recent years. This rail breaks that curve.

Near-zero chargebacks. A2A is irrevocable and SCA-authenticated in the bank. The scheme dispute path doesn't apply. Direct relief on Visa Acquirer Monitoring Programme (VAMP) exposure (Visa's 1.5% threshold took effect April 2026; acquirers held lower), fraud-team load, and dispute fees. iGaming card chargeback rates run well above mainstream retail; A2A removes the path.

RememberMe — faster repeat deposits. Returning players approve a deposit via the RememberMe token, which materially shortens the repeat-deposit flow versus a first-time journey. Token validity is 15 months. Every removed checkout step is a player who actually plays.

No checkout rebuild. Sits alongside cards as a new payment method. Theming and custom branding keep the experience inside your brand.

Where it runs

Built on Mastercard Open Banking rails:

  • 2,900+ banks connected across 19 European markets (UK + EU/EEA)
  • 99.99%+ infrastructure uptime — geo-redundant, auto failover, 24/7 support
  • PSD2 PISP framework — regulated rail, not a workaround

Bank depth varies per market. We confirm the top depositing banks for your priority markets up-front, before you commit.

What we cover today — and what we don't

Included today

  • Pay by Bank A2A deposits (customer → merchant)
  • Fixed-fee, tiered pricing structure
  • RememberMe token for repeat depositors
  • Theming and custom branding at checkout
  • Know Your Business (KYB) checks, integration, and go-live support
  • Callbacks, reporting, transaction monitoring

Not in scope today — be aware

  • Player payouts / withdrawals (merchant → customer). We cover the incoming side. The outbound flow is not productised yet.
  • Standalone recurring payments. Not possible under PSD2 as a standalone rail. Available in combination with Mastercard Payment Services in DK/NO.
  • Account information (AIS). We are PIS-only (Payment Initiation Services): no balance check, no Account Owner Verification (AOV), no account aggregation.
  • Refunds as standalone flow. Sits at platform level — confirmed case-by-case.

We are direct about what's in the box. If a feature isn't on this page, it isn't live with us today.
