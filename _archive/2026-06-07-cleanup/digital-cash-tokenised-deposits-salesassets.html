Your corporate treasurers are already asking

Tier-1 banks have launched tokenised deposits in production. Their corporate clients are seeing the difference in settlement speed, liquidity visibility, and 24/7 availability. The question moving through Tier-2 and Tier-3 boardrooms in 2026 is no longer whether to follow. It is how to stand a programme up before the corporate deposit base starts moving.

Under MiCA, EU banks can issue tokenised deposits under their existing licence, as e-money tokens or as deposit-token constructs aligned with their supervisory framework. The regulatory ramp is in place. The constraint is operational.

The shortcut to a credible programme

Banks that built internally took 12 to 24 months and five or more dedicated FTEs across custody, ledger, on-ramps, treasury integration, and compliance tooling. That runway is rarely available, and rarely the best use of internal engineering capacity.

Aryze delivers the operating layer as one coherent stack, so your team can focus on what is strategic: the use case, the customer experience, and the governance model.

What you get

Speed to a live programme. Phase 1 live within 4 to 6 months on a corporate pilot. Full institutional rollout across the following year.

Issuance under your own licence. Your deposit token, your terms, your customer relationship. Aryze provides the rails behind the issuance.

A coherent stack, not a vendor patchwork. Settlement, multi-currency support, governance hooks, AML and sanctions screening, audit trails, and integration with your core banking, TMS, ledger, and wallet systems. ISO 20022, FATF, and MiCA aligned.

On-ramps to and from the regulated banking system. Delivered through our Mastercard Open Banking partnership, with reach across 2,900+ banks in 19 European markets.

A partnership model that respects institutional governance. Multi-year framework with 24/7 institutional support, audit support, and a clear migration path if you ever need to move components in-house.
