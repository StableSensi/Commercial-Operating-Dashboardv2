Where your liquidity is actually sitting

Intraday liquidity is parked in nostro accounts, suspense buffers, and counterparty pre-funding because settlement still happens on T+1 or T+2 windows that were set decades ago. Reserve handling depends on end-of-day reconciliation that hides both surplus and shortfall during the trading day. Multi-currency positions require manual rebalancing across correspondent rails, with FX cost and timing risk attached to every move.

Cross-border correspondent banking still costs 5–8% in many corridors. The structural cost is capital trapped in the wrong place at the wrong time, not the headline fee.

A treasury layer that moves in real time

Tokenised treasury flows remove the settlement window from the value movement. Reserves and liquidity move on-ledger when you need them to move, with the position visible in real time, and with FX and multi-currency operations on a single rail.

Treasury teams that built this internally took 12 to 24 months and a fragmented vendor stack against the TMS, core banking, and ledger systems they already depend on. Aryze delivers the operating layer pre-built, with integration as part of the engagement, not as a post-deployment problem.

What you get

Real-time settlement and reserve handling tied to your underlying position, with intraday visibility instead of end-of-day reconciliation.

Multi-currency on one operating layer. Programmable FX, on-ledger rebalancing, and atomic settlement across currencies you already hold.

Programmable liquidity flows. Smart treasury operations under your governance: pre-defined approvals, controlled movement, audit trails on every transaction.

Issuance of the treasury asset under your own licence. Aryze provides the infrastructure; the regulated activity stays with you.

Integration with the TMS you already run. Kyriba, Bloomberg, in-house treasury systems, your core banking, ledger, and wallet stack. ISO 20022, FATF, and MiCA aligned. AML and sanctions screening built in.

On-ramps to and from the regulated banking system. Delivered through our Mastercard Open Banking partnership, with reach across 2,900+ banks in 19 European markets.
