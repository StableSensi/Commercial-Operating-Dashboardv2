Digital Cash

Operate digital cash under your own licence. Without building the operating layer yourself.

The position

Enterprise infrastructure for already-licensed EU institutions. Settlement, on-ramps, treasury, multi-currency, interoperability, governance hooks. Your institution remains issuer and operator.

The problem

Cross-border correspondent banking still costs 5–8% in many corridors, on T+2 settlement. Under MiCA, EU institutions can now run tokenised deposits and settlement assets under their own licence. The harder problem is not issuance. It is the operating layer behind it: settlement, liquidity, on-ramps, compliance, auditability, interoperability, reporting, and integration with the systems you already run.

Tier-1 banks have already moved. Tier-2 and Tier-3 institutions face the same question with a thinner runway.

The build trap

The institutions that have launched tokenised deposits built internally. The pattern is consistent: 12 to 24 months of programme time, five or more dedicated FTEs, and a fragmented vendor stack across custody, ledger, on-ramps, treasury integration, and compliance tooling. Each vendor brings its own integration risk and its own audit surface.

Most institutions cannot fund that runway. They also cannot afford to wait for a consortium to deliver on their timeline.

What Aryze provides

The operating layer. One coherent stack covering:

  • Settlement and clearing between participants
  • On-ramps via the Mastercard Open Banking partnership (2,900+ banks across 19 European markets, 99.99%+ uptime)
  • Treasury functionality, liquidity management, reserve handling
  • Multi-currency issuance on a single operating layer
  • Interoperability across networks and counterparties (private and public DLT)
  • Governance hooks: controls, approvals, audit trails that match your governance model
  • Integration with core banking, treasury management, ledger, and wallet systems
  • Standards alignment with ISO 20022, FATF, and MiCA where relevant
  • AML, KYC, and sanctions-screening hooks built in
  • Multi-year partnership with enterprise SLA and 24/7 institutional support

Where the line sits

Aryze is the infrastructure provider. By design:

  • Aryze does not hold your licence
  • Aryze is not the issuer
  • Aryze does not operate the programme

Your institution stays in control of the regulated activity. Aryze provides the rails behind it.

What a phased engagement looks like

Discovery and a working session with your treasury, legal, compliance, and technology stakeholders. Solution architecture against your existing core banking, TMS, ledger, and wallet stack. Phase 1 live within 4 to 6 months on a narrow, in-scope use case. The full operating model rolled out across the following year.

Institutional infrastructure runs on an institutional cycle. Total engagement is typically 6 to 18 months. It is not plug-and-play, and we will not pitch it as such.

Next step

A 30-minute conversation to scope your use case and confirm fit against your licence, your existing stack, and your timeline. We will come back with framing, not a quote, after that call.
