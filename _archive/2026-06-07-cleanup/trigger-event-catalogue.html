Cross-product triggers

Some events affect more than one product. Worth flagging:

  • EU Digital Euro project advancement — affects Digital Cash (institutional posture) and Stablecoin Factory (MiCA-aligned deployment).
  • US stablecoin legislation passing — affects Stablecoin Factory (US fintechs) and Digital Cash (US banks).
  • Crypto/iGaming regulatory consolidation — affects Pay by Bank (high-risk merchant pressure) and Stablecoin Factory (compliance-aligned deployment).

How to use this catalogue

  1. In outreach drafting: Open with the trigger, not the product. "With VAMP dropping to 1.5% in April, we're seeing iGaming operators re-evaluate their card-rails — we should talk." That beats "I wanted to introduce Aryze."
  2. In qualification: If you can't name a trigger, the lead is interest not urgency. Plan accordingly — slow nurture, not active sprint.
  3. In account planning: Triggers expire. The VAMP April 2026 trigger has a 6-month window before it becomes "old news." Pay by Bank pipeline built on it should close by Q4 2026 or move to the next trigger.
  4. In weekly review: New triggers fire weekly (regulator dialogue, peer announcements, job changes). Add a 15-minute scan to Monday mornings.
